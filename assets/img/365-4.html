Self-Expression -Day4

WEEK 1, DAY 4, SELF PORTRAIT
APRIL 14, 2016

Hey friends! Today I chose to share with you a poem written by Floetic that inspired this picture. It's a short entry today, but I hope it motivates you to express yourself in creative ways.

My mother introduced me to ballet when I was very young and I fell in love with the art of movement. Even though I don't actively practice it, it's still a very real part of me that most people don't know. It has helped me express myself when words are not enough. Just dancing and embracing your feelings while you dance is a powerful thing. I hope everyone has an activity like this to liberate yourself. I can't tell you how much it has helped me along the way. So, here you have it.

Self-expression
Through my art form I’ve learned a life long lesson
What comes from within
Is destined to materialize
But it is only through the words
That this occurs
And I prefer
To speak my peace
So I can find inner relief
Express my beliefs
And cope with the grief
Of my day to day life
Empowered and free
Acknowledged, heard and me
I feel as the words spill
From my lips
Like the drips of rainwater
Through a drain
As they stain this earth
With my existence
Because no one else has the capacity
To have the audacity to speak
What’s on my mind
And over time
I’ve learned this crucial message
Only through me
Along with the source of my creator
I’m the maker, risk taker
Earthquaker sending my current
Like a Doppler radar
And watching the effects
Trickle to those who need my being
Imagine a world without you
A world without me
Those in your life, friends and family
Defined without your reality
How scary would that be?
Because we are all necessary
Have been put here with a purpose
Meaning and significance
And there is a reason we’re all different
The same is lame
And embracing our distinctness
Is what makes us important
So I say express those vibes inside
Let them out, leak and release
Emancipate your humanity
And embody your individuality
Because in it’s totality
It is the gravity that keeps you grounded
And I have lost and found this
Time and again
But it’s a life long lesson I’m learning
Self-expression is our calling
So answer

If you haven't discovered something that you feel passionate about, I encourage you to find something that makes feel free. I'm possitive that it will improve your life in a million diferent ways!

Sincerely, Katia A. Hernandez
